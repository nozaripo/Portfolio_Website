About me

I am a creative researcher and a certified data scientist. I have 10+ years of experience in technical domains ranging from robotics and physics-based modeling to predictive quantitative modeling, data science & analytics, and AI/ML. I am an AI/Data enthusiast and enjoy turning complex technical problems into simple yet informative and actionable insights.

I hold a Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Southern California (USC), where the core scope of my research focused on the development of innovative predictive simulation platforms to understand human motor control. With a Master's and a Bachelor's degree, both in Mechanical Engineering, I have a background in robotics and the design of mechanical systems.

My passion is to build data/process models that drive actionable insights and predictions, addressing scientific challenges. My aim is to leverage my unique blend of diverse expertise to contribute to the ML/AI and data science fields as an AI engineer and a data scientist and forge a meaningful impact on human lives.
